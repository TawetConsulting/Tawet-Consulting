Credit Risk Modeling

Through introspection, advanced data analytics and experts' opinions, we develop credit risk models that scan prospective borrowers and predict the probability of default of

the loan by the borrower based on available data and the data provided by the borrower. We back-test this model and train a staff of your company on how to use the model, we also optimize the model with AI tools to provide improvement on the model with increased volume and variety in the present data.
